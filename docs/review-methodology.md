# How PHTAYA Reviews Gaming Platforms

PHTAYA follows a structured and neutral approach when reviewing or explaining online gaming platforms. The goal is to help users understand how platforms work before making any decisions.

## Core principles

All content published on PHTAYA is based on the following principles:

- Neutral and informational content
- Focus on platform structure and user experience
- No direct encouragement to register or place bets
- Clear separation between explanation and promotion

## Review criteria

When analyzing gaming platforms, PHTAYA focuses on:

- Platform navigation and usability
- Account structure and user flow
- Types of games or features available
- General transparency and clarity of information
- Responsible gaming context and legal considerations

## What PHTAYA does not do

PHTAYA does not:

- Offer betting advice or strategies
- Guarantee outcomes or profits
- Act as an operator or service provider
- Replace official platform terms or regulations

## Purpose of this documentation

This documentation exists to help users understand online gaming environments in a simple and accessible way, especially for beginners.

For official platform access and detailed policies, users should always refer to the official website.
