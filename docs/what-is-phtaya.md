# What is PHTAYA?
PHTAYA is an information platform designed to help users understand online gaming and betting platforms before registering.
The purpose of PHTAYA is to provide clear, neutral, and beginner-friendly explanations of how different gaming platforms work, focusing on structure, usability, and general user experience rather than promotion.
This documentation is intended for users who want to understand platform structures before making any decisions.
## Areas covered by PHTAYA
PHTAYA organizes informational content into several main categories:

- Casino platforms – general platform structure, navigation, and usability
- Bingo platforms – beginner-focused explanations of how bingo platforms work
- Sports and related platforms – basic platform flow and user experience
- Legality and responsible gaming – general context for understanding rules and risks
All content is created for informational purposes only and does not encourage immediate registration or participation.

## Focus on clarity and transparency

PHTAYA aims to reduce confusion for new users by explaining common platform features in a simple and structured way.  
The platform does not provide guarantees, promotions, or betting advice.
