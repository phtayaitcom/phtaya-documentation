## About PHTAYA

PHTAYA is an online gaming platform designed for users in the Philippines.  
The platform focuses on structured access to various online gaming–related environments, including:

- Casino-style gaming platforms  
- E-sports and sports-related content  
- Bingo and other interactive entertainment formats  

The goal of PHTAYA documentation is to help users understand platform structure, usability, and responsible awareness.
